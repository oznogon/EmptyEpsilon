HangarView populates from a list of docked MultiplayerObjectIds, stored in STBO and updated in SpaceShip's requestUndock and collide (when docking).

Selecting a ship lists and updates its hull, shield, systems damage, and weapons storage as key/value items. This list runs across two columns.

Global controls to toggle things like energy sharing go alongside the model view.

We should set and enforce docking ship count limits on the vector size, and/or measure impact on network.
